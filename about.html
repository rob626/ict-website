Our Story

Innovative Conference Technologies, LLC (ICT) is a boutique IT events services business that evolved over the last twenty plus years. All of our team members have been forged in the fire of delivering network, hardware deployment, digital signage, and other IT related, temporary event services when the clock is ticking. It takes a special group of people to plan and execute a functional work space within hours to be used for just a few days. This team has been doing so as corporate employees for decades and now as part of a tight-knit band of exceptionally talented individuals at ICT.
